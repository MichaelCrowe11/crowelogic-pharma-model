# CroweLogic Pharmaceutical AI Training Dataset v1.0.0

## Release Highlights

This is the initial public release of the CroweLogic Pharmaceutical AI Training Dataset, the industry's largest instruction-tuned pharmaceutical training corpus.

### Dataset Statistics

- **10,000,000** instruction-response pairs
- **3,332,908** Gold tier examples (90-99 quality score)
- **6,667,092** Silver tier examples (75-89 quality score)
- **1,000** unique synthetic compounds
- **12+** pharmaceutical domain areas
- **88.4/100** mean quality score

### What's Included

**Production Datasets:**
- `crowelogic_curated_full/crowelogic_gold.jsonl` (3.3GB)
- `crowelogic_curated_full/crowelogic_silver.jsonl` (6.4GB)

**Community Edition:**
- `community_edition/crowelogic_community_100k.jsonl` (28MB) - FREE

**Documentation:**
- Complete IP protection framework
- Quality scoring methodology
- Licensing and monetization strategy
- ORCID attribution

### Content Coverage

- Molecular properties (logP, TPSA, MW, formulas)
- Drug-likeness (Lipinski's Rule of Five)
- Pharmacokinetics (ADME properties)
- Clinical applications (dosing, safety)
- Drug discovery (SAR, target identification)
- Structural analysis (bonds, stereochemistry)

### Quality Assurance

Every example includes:
- Scientific accuracy scoring
- Linguistic quality assessment
- Training value metrics
- Cryptographic watermarking
- Proprietary compound tracking
